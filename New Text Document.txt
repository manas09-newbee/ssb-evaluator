### What the product actually is

**Interview Module (USP)**

```text
AI Interviewing Officer
        ↓
Asks question (voice + text)
        ↓
Candidate speaks answer
        ↓
Live speech-to-text on screen
        ↓
AI analyzes response
        ↓
AI asks cross-question
        ↓
Candidate answers again
        ↓
Dynamic interview conversation
        ↓
OLQ evaluation
        ↓
Final report
```

Typing is only:

```text
Fallback mode
```

not the main feature.

### PPDT USP

Not:

```text
Picture shown
Candidate types story
AI evaluates
```

Instead:

```text
Picture shown
        ↓
Candidate writes story on paper
        ↓
Uploads image/photo
        ↓
OCR extracts handwriting
        ↓
AI evaluates:
   • Story quality
   • Grammar
   • Handwriting readability
   • Officer Like Qualities
   • Positive/negative indicators
        ↓
Feedback report
```

That is much closer to an actual SSB preparation tool.
